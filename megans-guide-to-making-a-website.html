Online accounts/downloads

  1. make an account with cloudflare.com
  2. make an account with github.com
  3. download publii

Next Steps

  • Install Publii and open it
  • Go to GitHub and make a new repository
  • Configure publii to sync with your newly created repository. you may need this page: https://docs.github.com/en/authentication/keeping-your-account-and-data-secure/managing-your-personal-access-tokens
  • Connect cloudflare pages with your repository
  • Done! Your page should be available on the web!

Wrapping up

  • Buy a domain through cloudflare to customize your website address
  • Set up email with iCloud for free
